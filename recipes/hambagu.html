Japanese Hambagu

What is "hambagu"?

Hambāgu or Japanese Hamburger Steak, is a hugely popular dish in Japanese cuisine that is the ultimate comfort meal. It’s a steak patty made from ground meat; however, the dish is served with rice instead of buns. The style of meat is like Salisbury Steak or a single serving of meatloaf, but of course with a Japanese twist.

Ingredients:

  • 700g ground beef
  • 200g onion
  • 50g panko bread crumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbs oyster sauce
  • 1/3 ketchup
  • 1/4 ground nutmeg
  • Not full list. Please see for complete list.

Steps:

  1. Add beef, onion, panko, and egg to a bowl and mix with hands.
  2. Shape into small steaks.
  3. Place on baking sheet on aluminum foil.
  4. Mix remaining ingredients and pour over steaks.
  5. Bake at 360 degrees for 20 minutes.
  6. ENJOY ~
